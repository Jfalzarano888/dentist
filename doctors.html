Meet Dr. Grasso

Dr. Grasso graduated from Junita college in 1978 with a Bachelor’s in ________. To further his education, Dr. Grasso went to NYU College of Dentistry and graduated in 1981. After four years, Dr. Grasso opened up Hidden Lake Dental in 1985 and has been practicing ever since. Since opening Hidden Lake Dental, Dr. Grasso continued his education to improve his practice. He has taken numerous courses/seminars in crown and bridge, root canals, and cosmetic dentistry over the last 30 years.

Meet Dr. Kaga

Dr. Maha Kaga graduated from Rider University in 1996 with a Bachelor’s in Biology. She then went on to earn her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree in 2000 from the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine, after which she completed her general practice residency from Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital in 2001.

She currently lives in Middlesex County with her husband and 3 children.
